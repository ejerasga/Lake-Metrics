Lake Wood

Embrace Lake Wood's Beauty in Zamboanga del Sur

Nestled in the captivating province of Zamboanga del Sur, situated on the enchanting island of Mindanao in the southern Philippines, lies the serene and picturesque Lake Wood. Known by the indigenous Subanen people as "Danaw," this exquisite body of water graces the landscape, drawing both locals and travelers into its soothing embrace.
